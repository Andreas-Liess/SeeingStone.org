# Requirements: Privacy Page

## Job
Turn the old enterprise/security page into the Privacy page.

The page must feel fast.
Short lines.
Plain words.
No policy-speak.

## Core Message
SeeingStone finds the people inside your data.

Emails. PDFs. Attachments. Messages.
It links them to people, promises, dates, deals, and follow-ups.

## Audience
- Individuals
- Founders
- Startups
- Small teams
- Legacy SMEs with years of trapped business memory

## Privacy Message
Your database lives on your device.
We do not access it.
GDPR compliant.

Say:
- Database on your device.
- We do not access it.
- GDPR compliant.
- Small teams can keep company memory private.

Do not explain hardware setup on the site.

## Words To Use
- People layer
- Business memory
- Database on your device
- We do not access it
- GDPR compliant
- One place
- Old threads
- PDFs
- Attachments
- Clients
- Suppliers
- Follow-ups
- Promises
- What changed
- What to do next

## Words To Avoid
- Enterprise procurement
- Sales-led SaaS
- Usage-metered pricing
- Bespoke services
- Generic monitoring
- Professional-services niche language
- Corporate filler

## Page Structure

### Privacy Model
Cards:
1. For You
2. For Small Teams
3. All Your Sources

### How It Starts
Steps:
1. Ask for Access
2. Private Setup
3. Connect Sources
4. Use Daily

### CTA
"Get early access"
"Tell us what you want SeeingStone to remember."
